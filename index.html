اطّلع على سياسات السلامة

Fly_To Airline affirms its strong dedication to upholding the highest levels of aviation safety across all operations. Our Safety Management System (SMS) provides a structured framework that supports effective hazard detection, risk assessment, and the development of a proactive safety culture throughout the organization. Every employee, contractor, and partner is expected to adhere to these principles and contribute to a safe working environment.
Our commitments include:
• Maintaining full compliance with ICAO Annex 19 requirements, national regulatory frameworks, and recognized international standards.
• Supplying the workforce with the tools, training, and resources needed to effectively implement and continuously strengthen the SMS.
• Fostering an open-reporting culture where safety concerns can be raised confidently and without fear of blame or disciplinary action.
• Setting clear, measurable safety objectives and conducting routine reviews of performance indicators to ensure ongoing improvement.
• Supporting continuous learning through transparent communication, targeted training, and awareness initiatives that reinforce safety values.
• Ensuring that all risk-reduction strategies are practical, effective, and integrated seamlessly into everyday operational procedures.
At Fly_To Airline, safety is a shared duty. Through collaboration, accountability, and consistent application of safety principles, we aim to ensure that all activities are performed with the highest level of safety, efficiency, and operational integrity.

Air_To Airline operates a comprehensive Safety Assurance program to ensure the ongoing effectiveness of its Safety Management System (SMS) across all flight and operational activities. The Safety Department continuously monitors Safety Performance Indicators (SPIs) across critical airline functions, including flight operations, aircraft maintenance, crew management, ground handling, and in-flight services.
Regular safety audits, compliance checks, and trend analyses are conducted to verify adherence to internal procedures and regulatory requirements, while identifying areas for improvement.
All incidents are thoroughly investigated to determine root causes, and corrective and preventive actions are implemented. Safety reports and identified hazards are tracked to resolution within 30 days, ensuring accountability and timely mitigation. The findings from audits, reviews, and investigations are presented during quarterly safety meetings and communicated to senior management, supporting informed, data-driven decisions.
Through this continuous process of monitoring, evaluation, feedback, and improvement, Air_To Airline ensures that safety remains the cornerstone of all operations and a core value embedded in every department.

Air_To Airline fosters a strong and proactive safety culture through continuous education, transparent communication, and active employee engagement.
All airline personnel, including flight crews, maintenance teams, and ground staff, receive initial and recurrent safety training to ensure they remain competent, up-to-date, and fully aware of operational safety requirements across all airline operations.
Air_To maintains an open and non-punitive safety reporting system, allowing employees, contractors, and partners to report hazards, incidents, and near-misses without fear of reprisal.
Regular safety bulletins, team briefings, and Safety Awareness campaigns are used to share lessons learned, highlight recent safety achievements, and encourage proactive behavior throughout the organization.
A Safety Recognition Program acknowledges individuals and teams who make outstanding contributions to improving safety performance across the airline.
Through these continuous initiatives, Air_To ensures that safety awareness is actively promoted, widely shared, and deeply embedded as a core value within the company.
